Bones Ijeoma, the CEO and co-founder of AllSafe IT, is dedicated to changing the way IT services are managed. His goal is to eliminate downtime for businesses. Bones has a solid background in technology, with a BS in Computer Engineering from California State University, Long Beach, and an MBA in Entrepreneurship from the USC Marshall School of Business.

‍

His career before AllSafe IT included roles at well-known organizations like Marriott Hospitality, Dreamworks, and UCLA Medical Center. It was during this time that Bones saw a major need: small businesses didn't have easy access to advanced technology solutions that bigger companies use. This observation led him to start AllSafe IT, with a vision to provide small businesses with the technology support they need to compete effectively.

‍

Thanks to Bones' leadership, AllSafe IT is now a leading name in providing reliable and innovative IT solutions. He continues to guide the company with a focus on excellence, ensuring that AllSafe IT is a trusted partner for businesses looking to succeed in a digital world.
