## **What managed antivirus and cybersecurity services include**

Comprehensive cybersecurity services protect multiple attack surfaces simultaneously.

Endpoint protection secures all devices accessing your network. Laptops, desktops, mobile devices, and servers require consistent security. Managed antivirus deploys across endpoints, scans files and applications, blocks malicious software, and removes threats automatically. Protection extends to remote employees working from various locations.

Threat detection identifies attacks traditional antivirus misses. Endpoint detection and response monitors behavior, analyzes processes for suspicious activity, identifies ransomware attempts, and stops attacks before damage occurs. This catches threats that signature-based tools overlook.

Security controls enforce policies across your environment. Access management restricts who can reach sensitive data. Security policies define acceptable use. Control implementation ensures employees follow security practices. These controls reduce risk from both external threats and internal mistakes.

Compliance management aligns security with regulatory requirements. Healthcare organizations need HIPAA controls. Financial services require specific data protection. Our cybersecurity services implement necessary security capabilities, document compliance measures, and prepare for audits.

Vulnerability management identifies weaknesses before attackers exploit them. We scan systems regularly, prioritize risks based on business impact, patch critical vulnerabilities, and harden security settings. This proactive approach prevents many security breaches.
