Did you know that cloud migration has a lot of benefits for your business? [Cloud migration](https://www.techtarget.com/searchcloudcomputing/definition/cloud-migration) is the actual process of transitioning from databases into the cloud. It allows businesses to focus on their growth as they work within the existing infrastructure. Your data can grow exponentially without affecting your operations’ performance.  
Our team at AllSafe IT knows how crucial it is for businesses, that’s why we always aim to provide the best cloud migration solutions to our clients. Our ability to deliver sustainable results has helped us establish a name for ourselves here in Los Angeles.  
That being said, we are glad to finally receive our first review on Clutch, a Washington, DC.-based B2B review agency. The platform is widely respected in the B2B space for its commitment to flagging fake reviews, sketchy vendors, and curating helpful content.  
For our first review, a real estate company enlisted us to help with their cloud migration and provide [IT consulting](https://www.allsafeit.com/services/it-consulting) services. An analyst from Clutch personally interviewed the client over the phone to ask for their feedback.

![clutch review](/images/blog/allsafe-it-receives-first-review-on-clutch/clutch-review.png)

I don’t have any complaints about their attitude.”
