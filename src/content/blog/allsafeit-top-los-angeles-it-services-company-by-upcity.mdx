AllSafe IT is an IT [services, consulting, and IT support firm](https://www.allsafeit.com/services/managed-it) with a dedicated, certified team of technology experts. Our goal is to create a partnership with your business that can support your immediate goals and give you the freedom, and IT infrastructure to plan for the future.This is why our Los Angeles-based company has been recognized by UpCity as not only one of  the best IT services companies in our area but also [nationally in the United States](https://www.upcity.com/it-services/los-angeles?spotlight=profiles%2Fallsafe-it%2Flos-angeles).

UpCity is a resource that helps connect businesses to service providers they can trust. With more than 50,000 listed providers—from branding agencies to accounting firms to HR consultants to IT specialists, and many more—1.5 million businesses (and counting) have visited UpCity to research and identify the best partner for their needs.

UpCity’s proprietary algorithm utilizes a variety of digital signals to measure the credibility, trustworthiness, and recommendability of B2B service providers across the U.S. and Canada. These digital data points like reviews (both on UpCity and other third-party review sites), presence in relevant search results, domain authority, website speed and experience help UpCity connect businesses with outstanding service providers like AllSafe IT. Dan Olson, CEO of UpCity, had this to say about the AllSafe IT team:

#### **“AllSafe IT knows that every minute of ‘down-time’ for your network equates to hundreds of dollars lost. As a result, they value a high response rate to ensure that you are covered 24/7. This dedication to the customer is why UpCity has highlighted AllSafe IT as a top provider.”**
