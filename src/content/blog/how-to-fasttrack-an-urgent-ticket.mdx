You can flag a service ticket for escalation if it needs urgent attention. This is called FastTrack. FastTracked tickets will automatically be escalated in priority, and our Help Desk team will be alerted so the ticket can be assigned to a technician right away.

To FastTrack a ticket, follow the steps below:

1.  Login to the [AllSafe App](https://app.allsafeit.com) and go to **Ticket Updates**.

![Asit fasttrack001](/images/blog/how-to-fasttrack-an-urgent-ticket/asit-fasttrack001.jpeg)

  
‍

2.  Find the ticket that you would like to FastTrack and click on it to view its details.

![Asit fasttrack002](/images/blog/how-to-fasttrack-an-urgent-ticket/asit-fasttrack002.jpeg)

‍

3.  Click the **Ticket options drop down** at the top of the ticket’s **Discussion** tab.

![Asit fasttrack003](/images/blog/how-to-fasttrack-an-urgent-ticket/asit-fasttrack003.jpeg)

‍

4.  Select the **FastTrack (flag ticket as urgent)** option.

![Asit fasttrack004](/images/blog/how-to-fasttrack-an-urgent-ticket/asit-fasttrack004.jpeg)

‍

5.  Click **OK** to confirm.

![Asit fasttrack005](/images/blog/how-to-fasttrack-an-urgent-ticket/asit-fasttrack005.jpeg)

‍

6.  The ticket priority will be escalated, and the Help Desk team will be alerted.

You can confirm that the escalation request went through by checking the ticket’s **Details** tab. The ticket status will be changed to “Client FastTrack Requested” and the priority will be changed to P2 | Quick Response.

![Asit fasttrack006](/images/blog/how-to-fasttrack-an-urgent-ticket/asit-fasttrack006.jpeg)
