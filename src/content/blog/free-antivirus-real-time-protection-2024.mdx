## **What is antivirus software and why is it essential?**

Antivirus software is a piece of software designed to detect, prevent, and remove malicious software, commonly known as malware, from your computer or mobile device. It acts as a security tool that protects your system from viruses, ransomware, spyware, and other types of malicious software. 

With the increasing sophistication of cyber threats, antivirus protection is no longer optional but a necessity for anyone who uses a computer, especially if you’re using the internet for sensitive activities like online banking or shopping.

In 2024, the need for [**antivirus with real-time protection**](https://www.cisa.gov/news-events/news/understanding-anti-virus-software) has never been greater. Real-time protection ensures that any suspicious activity on your computer is immediately flagged and dealt with before it can cause any harm. 

Whether you’re using Windows 10, Windows 11, or another version of Windows, the right antivirus solution can protect your Windows PC from various cyber threats.

## **Types of antivirus software**

Understanding the different types of antivirus programs can help you choose the best antivirus software for your needs. Here’s a breakdown of the main types:

### **1\. Standalone antivirus**

Standalone antivirus software is designed to focus solely on virus protection. It doesn’t include extra features like internet security or identity theft protection, but it excels at what it’s meant to do—detecting and removing viruses. 

This type of antivirus program is ideal for users who want a lightweight solution that doesn’t take up too many system resources.

### **2\. Internet security suites**

Internet security suites offer more comprehensive protection than standalone antivirus software. In addition to virus protection, they include features like firewall protection, identity theft protection, and internet security tools. 

These suites are perfect for users who want a complete security solution that covers all aspects of their online activity.

### **3\. Antivirus with real-time protection**

**Antivirus with real-time protection** constantly monitors your system for any suspicious activity. Unlike traditional antivirus programs that require manual scans, real-time protection ensures that threats are detected and neutralized as they occur. 

This type of software is essential for users who frequently download files, browse the internet, or engage in online transactions.

### **4\. Free antivirus software**

Free antivirus software offers basic [protection against viruses and malware](https://www.allsafeit.com/services/cybersecurity) without the cost. While they might lack some advanced features found in paid antivirus solutions, many free antivirus programs offer excellent real-time protection. 

These are ideal for users looking for a good free antivirus solution that offers adequate security without needing to invest in premium antivirus software.

### **5\. Paid antivirus software**

Paid antivirus software provides a more comprehensive level of protection, including advanced features like ransomware protection, identity theft protection, and premium support. These programs are often better suited for businesses or users who need the highest level of security. 

While free versions of antivirus software are good, paid antivirus subscriptions offer enhanced protection and additional features that are worth considering if you have more advanced security needs.

## **What does antivirus with real-time protection do?**

**Antivirus with real-time protection** is designed to continuously monitor your computer or mobile device for any suspicious activity. Unlike traditional antivirus programs that require you to manually initiate a scan, real-time protection works in the background, scanning files and applications as they are accessed or downloaded.

## **How real-time protection works**

**Antivirus with real-time protection** is crucial because it stops threats before they can infect your system. Whether you’re downloading a file, browsing a website, or installing a new application, real-time protection ensures that you’re covered.

1.  **Monitoring**: Real-time protection constantly monitors all system activities, including file transfers, downloads, and program installations. This ensures that any [potentially harmful actions](https://www.allsafeit.com/services/cybersecurity/managed-firewall) are detected immediately.
2.  **Detection**: When a file or application is accessed, the antivirus software compares it against a database of known threats. If the file matches a signature in the database, it’s flagged as a threat.
3.  **Prevention**: The software then takes action to prevent the threat from causing any damage. This could involve quarantining the file, deleting it, or blocking the application from running.
4.  **Updates**: Real-time protection relies on up-to-date threat databases. The best antivirus software frequently updates its database to include the latest known threats, ensuring that your device is protected against even the newest forms of malware.
5.  **User alerts**: If a threat is detected, the antivirus software will notify you immediately, allowing you to take further action if necessary.
