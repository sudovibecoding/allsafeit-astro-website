## How Does A VPN Work?

![How Does A VPN Work](/images/blog/best-free-vpns-for-windows/how-does-a-vpn-work-1024x536.jpeg)

VPN stands for Virtual Private Network. When you do not use a VPN, the computer’s IP address is used to send the webpage request to the webserver. Therefore, it is easy for anyone to track your computer’s IPS and use it to identify you.

However, when you use a VPN, your computer’s IP address is rerouted and it goes to the VPN’s server. Here a cover-up IP address is assigned to the request and the required web page is fetched. This way, the website you access cannot see your computer’s actual IP address thus protecting your online identity completely. All the information that you enter on the site is also encrypted thus providing a comprehensive and all around layer of security.
