**NEWPORT BEACH, California — October 10, 2025 —** AllSafe IT officially expanded its Southern California footprint with a new office in **Newport Beach, Orange County**, enhancing local access to managed IT services, cybersecurity support, and IT response. This new location brings the team closer to businesses across the Irvine–Anaheim–Newport Beach corridor, enabling faster service delivery and stronger partnerships with organizations that rely on technology uptime and secure infrastructure.

## **Serving Orange County SMBs with Faster, Local IT Support**

Strategically located near John Wayne Airport and the Irvine Business District, the new office allows AllSafe IT to dispatch engineers more quickly and provide hands-on support for small and mid-sized businesses that value rapid IT response times and proactive cybersecurity defense.

Businesses in Orange County can now access dedicated local [IT support](https://www.allsafeit.com/services/managed-it/help-desk), [IT consulting](https://www.allsafeit.com/services/it-consulting), and [cybersecurity services](https://www.allsafeit.com/services/cybersecurity) backed by a team familiar with the region’s fast-moving business environment.

_Looking for reliable_ **_IT Support in Orange County_**_?_ Learn more here: [**AllSafe IT – IT Support Orange County**](https://www.allsafeit.com/it-support-orange-county)

## **Why This Expansion Matters for Local Businesses**

With long-standing operations in **Pasadena (Headquarters)** and **Hollywood**, AllSafe IT has already served businesses across Los Angeles with proactive IT support and cybersecurity expertise. Expanding into **Orange County** builds on this foundation by creating a **closer, more responsive service hub** specifically for organizations located south of LA, reducing travel time and improving on-site availability.

This new office empowers us to deliver true local IT coverage, not just remote support, with SLA-backed response times designed around the operational needs of Newport Beach, Costa Mesa, Irvine, Anaheim, and surrounding business communities. From professional service firms and financial offices to logistics centers and growing SMBs, businesses in this region can now access faster, localized [IT support in Orange County](https://www.allsafeit.com/it-support-orange-county) without the delays of LA-based dispatching.
