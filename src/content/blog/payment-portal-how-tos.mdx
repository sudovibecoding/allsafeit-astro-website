1.  Login to the AllSafe App and click **Account > Invoices**

- Enter your work email address and click **Continue**.
- You’ll receive an email from the the email address "noreply@notifications.alternativepayments.io".
- Click the link in the email to confirm your email and login.

![Allsafe it alternativepayments001](/images/blog/payment-portal-how-tos/Allsafe it-alternativepayments001.jpeg)

‍

2.  If you don't see the login email right away, please check your spam/junk folders and wait a few minutes before clicking the reset link. You may need to whitelist your email address.
3.  Once you're logged in, click on the **Outstanding Payments** menu option.

- Select the invoice you want to pay
- Select **Pay** on the invoice you want to pay, or select **Pay other invoice** and enter the invoice information to make a payment.

![Allsafe it alternativepayments002](/images/blog/payment-portal-how-tos/Allsafe it-alternativepayments002.jpeg)

‍

- Click the button to **Review & pay invoice**.

![Allsafe it alternativepayments003](/images/blog/payment-portal-how-tos/Allsafe it-alternativepayments003.jpeg)

‍

3.  Choose your preferred payment method

- Select your preferred payment method and click **Continue**.

![Allsafe it alternativepayments004](/images/blog/payment-portal-how-tos/Allsafe it-alternativepayments004.jpeg)

‍

- To pay your invoice over time, select **Pay in installments** and choose payment terms of 30-150 days on the left side of the screen, with equal installments due each month until the invoice has been paid in full. Alternative’s fee of 2% to 6%, subject to your preferred duration, will be included in the monthly payment. See your payment schedule for more details. Pay in installments is a great option for larger invoices, to help manage cash flow.

![Allsafe it alternativepayments005](/images/blog/payment-portal-how-tos/Allsafe it-alternativepayments005.jpeg)

‍

4.  Connect your bank account or credit card and submit your payment

- To connect your bank account, click **Connect through Plaid** and follow the instructions.

![Allsafe it alternativepayments006](/images/blog/payment-portal-how-tos/Allsafe it-alternativepayments006.jpeg)

‍

- If your bank is not supported by Plaid, click **Connect manually** to enter your bank details manually.

![Allsafe it alternativepayments007](/images/blog/payment-portal-how-tos/Allsafe it-alternativepayments007.jpeg)

‍

- If you are paying via credit card, add your card details and click **Add Card** and enter your card information.

![Allsafe it alternativepayments008](/images/blog/payment-portal-how-tos/Allsafe it-alternativepayments008.jpeg)

‍

- Now you’re ready to pay an invoice!
