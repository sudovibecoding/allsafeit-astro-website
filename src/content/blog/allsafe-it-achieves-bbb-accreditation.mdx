### **About the Better Business Bureau (BBB)**

The [Better Business Bureau (BBB)](https://www.allsafeit.com/infrastructure-and-cloud/cloud-migration-and-integration-los-angeles) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing marketplace trust by setting standards for ethical business behavior and monitoring compliance. For over 100 years, the BBB has helped consumers identify trustworthy companies through independent accreditation, public business profiles, and a transparent rating system. Businesses that earn BBB Accreditation commit to operating with honesty, accountability, and a high level of customer care, allowing customers to make confident decisions when selecting service providers.
