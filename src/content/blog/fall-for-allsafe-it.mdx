At AllSafe IT, we are constantly striving to provide you with the best support experience possible. Your feedback plays a crucial role in helping us understand your needs and improve our services. That's why we're launching our **Fall for AllSafe IT** promotion, and we invite you to be a part of it!

Here's how it works:

1.  Every time we close a support ticket, you will receive an email with a quick survey to rate your experience.
2.  All you have to do is click on the corresponding icon to indicate whether your interaction was positive, neutral, or negative.

![summer survey sweepstakes 01](/images/blog/fall-for-allsafe-it/summer-survey-sweepstakes-01.jpeg)

1.  Leave a comment explaining your sentiment. If you chose a neutral or negative option, we would love to understand the reasons behind your selection and hear your suggestions on how we can enhance our services to better meet your needs. **_Only feedback with comments will be entered in the sweepstakes_**.

![summer survey sweepstakes 02](/images/blog/fall-for-allsafe-it/summer-survey-sweepstakes-02.jpeg)

As a token of our appreciation for your time and effort, all clients who provide commented feedback will be automatically entered into a monthly raffle for a chance to win a $100 Amazon gift card. It's our way of saying thank you for helping us serve you better!

The Fall for AllSafe IT promotion will run throughout the fall, from October through December 2023. At the end of each month, we will randomly select one lucky winner. So keep an eye on your inbox after each support ticket closure, and be sure to click, rate, and comment! The more feedback you provide, the more chances you have to win!

### Official Contest Rules:

-   Eligibility:  
    \- The Fall for AllSafe IT promotion (the "Contest") is open to all clients of AllSafe IT who have received a support ticket closure email with a survey during the contest period (October 1, 2023 - December 31, 2023). Participants must be at least 18 years of age or the legal age of majority in their jurisdiction, whichever is higher, at the time of entry.  
    \- Employees, representatives, and immediate family members of AllSafe IT are not eligible to participate in the Contest.
-   Contest Period:  
    \- The Contest begins on October 1, 2023, at 12:00 a.m. PT and ends on December 31, 2023, at 11:59 p.m. PT. All entries must be received within this period to be eligible for the Contest.
-   How to Enter:  
    \- Participants will automatically receive an entry into the Contest when they provide commented feedback in response to the survey included in the support ticket closure email. Each completed survey with a comment will count as one entry. Only one entry will be accepted per support ticket.  
    \- No purchase or payment is necessary to enter the Contest.
-   Winner Selection:  
    \- At the end of each month during the contest period, AllSafe IT will randomly select one winner from the pool of eligible entries received that month.  
    \- The random selection will be conducted using an unbiased, computerized system.  
    \- The odds of winning depend on the total number of eligible entries received each month.
-   Prize:  
    \- Each winner will receive a $100 Amazon gift card.  
    \- The prize is non-transferable, and no cash alternatives will be offered.  
    \- AllSafe IT reserves the right to substitute the prize with an item of equal or greater value if necessary.
-   Winner Notification:  
    \- The selected winner will be notified by email within seven (7) days following the end of each month during the contest period.
-   Prohibited Actions:  
    \- Participants are prohibited from creating fake or "dummy" support tickets solely for the purpose of entering the Contest. Any such entries will be deemed ineligible, and participants found engaging in this activity may be disqualified from the Contest at the discretion of AllSafe IT.
-   General Conditions:  
    \- By entering the Contest, participants agree to abide by these official contest rules and the decisions of AllSafe IT, which are final and binding on all matters relating to the Contest.  
    \- AllSafe IT reserves the right to disqualify any participant found to be violating these rules or tampering with the entry process.  
    \- AllSafe IT is not responsible for lost, late, incomplete, or misdirected entries or for any technical or human errors that may occur in the entry process or the administration of the Contest.
-   Privacy:  
    \- Any personal information collected from participants will be used solely for the purpose of administering the Contest and will be handled in accordance with AllSafe IT's privacy policy.
-   Governing Law and Jurisdiction:  
    \- The Contest and these official contest rules shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of California.
